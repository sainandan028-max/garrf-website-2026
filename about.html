About the Foundation

The GopalKrishna Advanced Rural Research Foundation (GARRF) is a non-profit research organization committed to bridging advanced scientific research with sustainable rural development. We promote interdisciplinary innovation through Artificial Intelligence, Structural Health Monitoring, Smart Villages, Capacity Building, and Women Empowerment.

Our mission is to nurture future scientists, support student research, develop innovative technologies, and create practical solutions that positively impact rural communities worldwide.

Our Story

The Gopalkrishna Advanced Rural Research Foundation (GARRF) was born from a shared dream of a father and son who believe that scientific research should ultimately serve society. Their journey spans continents, generations, and experiences, yet remains united by one purpose—using knowledge and innovation to improve lives, particularly in rural communities.

Their story began in Singapore, where Shantanu spent his early childhood immersed in the academic environment surrounding Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Growing up amidst researchers, laboratories, and an international culture of innovation nurtured his curiosity and imagination. Fascinated by robotics, animation, artificial intelligence, engineering, football, and travel, he developed an early passion for understanding how technology can solve real-world problems. His robotics project later received recognition at school, reinforcing his enthusiasm for innovation.

As the family returned to India, they embraced a new vision—combining global research experience with local community needs. Travelling across different regions strengthened their belief that sustainable development begins by understanding people, their challenges, and their aspirations. This journey inspired a long-term commitment to creating technologies that are practical, affordable, and beneficial for rural Bharat.

A defining milestone came in April 2026, when Dr. Annamdas Venu Gopal Madhav received the prestigious Yahokirti Award from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Alumni Association in recognition of his research contributions. During this occasion, father and son reaffirmed their shared commitment to dedicate their knowledge, experience, and passion towards building an institution that promotes research, innovation, education, and service to society. That vision became the foundation of GARRF.

Today, GARRF represents a bridge between international research excellence and India's aspirations for sustainable development. Through interdisciplinary research, education, mentorship, artificial intelligence, structural health monitoring, smart infrastructure, and rural innovation, the Foundation seeks to empower future scientists while creating meaningful societal impact.

About Dr. Annamdas Venu Gopal Madhav

Dr. Annamdas Venu Gopal Madhav serves as the Chief Scientific Officer of GARRF. He earned his Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, holds a Master of Engineering degree from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, completed a MicroMasters programme through the Rochester Institute of Technology (USA), and is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP).

Over the past two decades, he has been associated with NTU Singapore as a student, researcher, scientist, faculty member, and visiting scholar. His professional journey also includes academic and research experience in Pittsburgh, USA. His research interests include Structural Health Monitoring, Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twins, Smart Infrastructure, Non-Destructive Evaluation, Sensors, Machine Learning, and Sustainable Engineering.

His long-term vision is to establish GARRF as a globally respected research organisation that nurtures young innovators, strengthens international collaborations, promotes interdisciplinary research, and develops affordable technologies that improve lives and contribute to the sustainable development of Bharat.
