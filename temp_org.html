About Our Organization

Building a Global Research Ecosystem for Rural Innovation

The Gopalkrishna Advanced Rural Research Foundation (GARRF) is an independent non-profit research organization dedicated to advancing scientific research, innovation, education and sustainable rural development. Guided by experienced academicians, scientists, engineers, industry professionals and young innovators, GARRF serves as a collaborative platform connecting academia, industry, government and society.

Our multidisciplinary research spans Artificial Intelligence, Structural Health Monitoring, Digital Twins, Robotics, Smart Villages, Sustainable Infrastructure, Renewable Energy, Healthcare, Water Resources and Capacity Building. Through national and international collaborations, GARRF aims to nurture future scientists while transforming research into meaningful societal impact for rural communities.

Leadership

Visionary leadership driving innovation, research and community development.

Dr. Venu Gopal Madhav Annamdas

Founder & Chief Scientific Officer (CSO)

M.E. (Indian Institute of Science Bangalore)
MicroMasters (Rochester Institute of Technology, USA)
Ph.D. (Nanyang Technological University, Singapore)
PMP® – Project Management Professional

Dr. Venu Gopal Madhav Annamdas has over two decades of international academic, industrial and research experience spanning India, Singapore and the United States. Throughout his career he has served as a student, researcher, scientist and visiting scholar while working closely with globally renowned institutions including the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) and research organizations in Singapore.

His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Structural Health Monitoring, Digital Twins, Smart Infrastructure, Sustainable Engineering and Rural Technology Innovation. In April 2026, he was honoured with the Yahokirti Award by the Indian Institute of Science Bangalore Alumni Association in recognition of his research contributions. This milestone inspired the establishment of GARRF with a vision to nurture future scientists and create meaningful societal impact through research.
